Pen: Pokemon Card Holo Effect

by Simon Goellner

"Pokemon Card Holo Effect" by Simon Goellener created a holographic effect for Pokemon cards when hovered over.

Goellner uses animation and effects to recreate the experience of holding a holographic Pokemon card. The card turns at different angles and changes colors based on where the pointer hovers over the card.

See the Pen Pokemon Card Holo Effect by Simon Goellner (@simeydotme) on CodePen.
